Description

This particular course is developed within EY and is a practical training in learning how to bring awareness, acceptance and non-judgment to your moment to moment experience. It makes use of a standard and tested format of 8-weeks mindfulness training that has proven to be successful in dissolving feelings of stress, anxiety and unhappiness. Due to the elements of emotional intelligence covered, it has also been shown to enhance performance by improving self-awareness, concentration, creativity, self-confidence, optimism, and empathy. The theory and practices are modelled on Mindfulness-based Stress Reduction (MBSR) and Mindfulness-based Cognitive Therapy (MBCT) with elements from Google's Search Inside Yourself Leadership training.

 

Excersises

Formal practice

  • Go through the week 1 pages of the Home Practice Handbook
  • Do the guided audio practice (Body Scan, 15 to 20 minutes) six days out of seven.

Informal practice

  • Eat one meal (or one bite) mindfully
  • Attempt to solve the 9 dots exercise, while noticing how you go about solving it

Insight practice

  • Keeping a daily log of your practice (use the form in your Home Practice Handbook) to record your experience
  • Identify your intentions for this course
